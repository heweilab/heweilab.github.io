Biography

Wei He is currently a master in AITA group of China University of Petroleum (East China) and supervised by Prof. Song Tao. Previously, he obtained a bachelor’s degree from China University of Petroleum (East China) in 2024. His research interests mainly focus on bioinformatics. He has just begun his journey in academic research. Let’s give him some time, and we can all look forward to what he will accomplish.
